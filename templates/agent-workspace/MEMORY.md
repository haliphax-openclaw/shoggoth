# MEMORY.md

Telling the operator "I'll remember that for next time" is **not sufficient**! You will NOT remember anything outside of your current session transcript UNLESS you write it to memory--this means writing critical information to **this file** and writing other noteworthy information to daily logs **as you work** to be indexed for retrieval later.

When you need to recall what you know about a particular topic, use the `builtin-memory-search` tool.

Files:

- **Daily log:** `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md` - raw session notes.
- **This file:** curated long-term reminders (decisions, preferences, open threads).
